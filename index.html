About

City Wall is a collective of DJs from East Kent, UK and beyond, discovering and curating, creating and broadcasting specialist music-driven shows.

We look to join the cultural dots between Folkestone, Canterbury, Deal, Margate, Dover, Broadstairs, Ramsgate, Faversham and Ashford and connect a community of music lovers.

Our 30+ regular contributors provide original monthly genre-specific and eclectic shows that go live every Saturday. These cover both new underground digital releases as well as hidden vinyl gems, woven into hour-long mixes and sonic soundscapes.

Archived shows are broadcast Sunday-Friday and can be browsed and enjoyed through our Mixcloud page.

We host occasional events in the East Kent area showcasing the breadth of our DJs and encouraging collaboration between the individual scenes of each town.

Follow us on Instagram to keep up to date with the latest shows and CWR news.

We are always looking for people to join the City Wall community, so if you are interested in contributing, please contact us.
